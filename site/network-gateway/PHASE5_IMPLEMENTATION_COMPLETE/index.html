    ✅ Pilot Deployment Plan¶

    Location: docs/network-gateway/pilot-deployment-plan.md

    Coverage: 1. Rollout Phases (4 phases) - Phase A: Internal Alpha — 20 employees, 1 week (Feb 10-16) - Phase B: Pilot Beta — 100 external users, 4 weeks (Feb 17-Mar 16) - Phase C: Limited GA — 1,000 users, 2 weeks (Mar 24-Apr 6) - Phase D: Full GA — Unrestricted, launch April 7, 2026

    1. Pilot Cohort Design
    2. Regional distribution: Lagos (40), Ibadan (40), Osogbo (20)
    3. Device mix: 60 eSIM smartphones, 40 physical SIM feature phones

    4. User selection criteria: Active learners, diverse age/geography, feedback willingness

    5. Success Metrics & KPIs (5 categories)

    6. Activation: Success rate ≥95%, time to activate P95 <90s, abandonment <10%
    7. Network Performance: Attach success ≥95%, Aku edge selection ≥70%, handover success ≥90%
    8. Policy & Billing: Enforcement accuracy 100%, reconciliation accuracy 100%
    9. User Experience: CSAT ≥4.0/5.0, DAU ≥70%, NPS ≥40

    10. Operational: Support first-contact resolution ≥80%, ticket volume <5/100 users/week, uptime 99.5%

    11. Monitoring & Observability Setup

    12. Grafana dashboards: Pilot KPI overview, real-time activation monitoring, billing audit (3 dashboards)
    13. Prometheus alerts: Critical (PagerDuty), high (Slack), medium (email) — 6 alert rules

    14. Event bus monitoring: Critical events (real-time), audit events (daily review)

    15. Rollback Procedures (3 plans)

    16. Plan A: Pause new activations (feature flag, 2-24 hour pause)
    17. Plan B: Deactivate pilot cohort (SMS notification, 2-8 hour downtime)

    18. Plan C: Full rollback (database restore, 8-48 hour downtime, data loss up to 24 hours)

    19. Risk Mitigation Strategies (4 major risks)

    20. HLR/HSS overload: Pre-pilot load testing, horizontal scaling, circuit breaker, backup HLR
    21. Policy enforcement failure: Daily audit, validation tests, alerts, manual review
    22. User churn: Weekly surveys, dedicated support, proactive outreach, incentives

    23. Support overwhelmed: Pre-pilot training, escalation path, FAQ, auto-routing

    24. Communication Plan

    25. Internal: Weekly pilot standups, monthly executive briefs
    26. External: Pre-pilot email, weekly SMS, week 2/4 surveys, post-pilot thank you

    27. Public: Status page, GA blog post, social media (Twitter, Instagram, Facebook)

    28. Go/No-Go Checklists (3 decision points)

    29. Phase A → B (Feb 16): Activation ≥90%, zero P1 incidents, support resolution ≥80%
    30. Phase B → C (Mar 23): Activation ≥95%, CSAT ≥4.0, DAU ≥70%, policy accuracy 100%

    31. Phase C → D (Apr 6): Activation ≥97%, API latency <2s, HLR CPU <70%, uptime ≥99.5%

    32. Post-GA Monitoring (Ongoing)

    33. Week 1-4: Daily reviews (stability, scaling, incidents)
    34. Month 2-3: Bi-weekly reviews (retention, revenue, optimizations)
