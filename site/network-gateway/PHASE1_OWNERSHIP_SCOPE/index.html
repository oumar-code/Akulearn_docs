    Scope Freeze — eSIM and Physical SIM Parity¶

    Policies (FROZEN)¶

    • ✅ Network selection: prefer Aku edge/super-hub, fallback to partner MNO
    • ✅ Policy delivery: OTA for eSIM, USSD/SMS for physical SIM
    • ✅ Retry/backoff: shared budget and exponential backoff
    • ✅ Attach/registration: common auth flows with device attestation

    Billing & Rating (FROZEN)¶

    • ✅ Prepaid/postpaid bundles: identical across SIM types
    • ✅ Zero-rated education content: same allow-list
    • ✅ Local breakout pricing: consistent in-state discounts
    • ✅ Roaming/interconnect: MNO fallback pricing parity

    Observability (FROZEN)¶

    • ✅ KPIs: activation success %, attach/registration success, breakout share, swap MTTR
    • ✅ Events: profile/ICCID lifecycle, policy push, attach/detach, SIM swap
    • ✅ Logs: audit trail for provisioning, CDRs for calls/SMS/data
    • ✅ Dashboards: unified view across eSIM and physical SIM

    Out of Scope (Deferred to Phase 3+)¶

    • ❌ Multi-SIM/eSIM profile stacking
    • ❌ Consumer self-service SIM swap (field agent only for pilot)
    • ❌ International roaming beyond MNO fallback
    • ❌ 5G SA core integration (4G/LTE focus for pilot)
