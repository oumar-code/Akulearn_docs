Pricing FAQs

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?
Yes. You can cancel your Pro or School subscription at any time from your account settings. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.
How do I pay? Is Paystack/Flutterwave supported?
We support all major Nigerian payment methods including Paystack, Flutterwave, bank transfer, and USSD payments. No foreign card required.
What happens when my free limit runs out?
Your access to past questions is paused until the next month, but you can upgrade to Pro at any time to get unlimited access immediately.
Is the annual plan significantly cheaper?
Yes! The annual Pro plan is billed at ₦21,000 per year (equivalent to ₦1,750/month) — a saving of 30% compared to monthly billing.
Can a school pay by invoice?
Absolutely. Schools and institutions can request invoice-based billing. Contact our sales team via the School plan button above and we'll set it up for you.
