## 🛠️ Content Generation Strategy

### Approach 1: AI-Powered (Fastest)
Using existing infrastructure (Google Notebook LM, Hugging Face, LLaMA):

**Step 1**: Feed past JAMB papers (2015-2025) → AI extracts topics
**Step 2**: Use EnhancedContentGenerator to create lesson notes
**Step 3**: Generate 10 variations per question (AI diversity)
**Step 4**: Human QA validates (2-3 days, 1 person)

**Output**: 2,000 new questions in 1 week
**Cost**: ~₦0-5,000 (compute credits)
**Quality**: 80-90% (needs review)

### Approach 2: Expert-Led (Best Quality)
Hire subject matter experts:

**Step 1**: Hire 2-3 Nigerian exam tutors (₦50K each/month)
**Step 2**: Create 500 questions each (high quality)
**Step 3**: Videos & explanations included
**Step 4**: Peer review across tutors

**Output**: 1,500 questions in 2 weeks
**Cost**: ~₦100K (expert fees)
**Quality**: 95%+ (tested approaches)

### Approach 3: Hybrid (Recommended)
**Step 1**: Use AI to generate 3,000 question variants
**Step 2**: Tutors review top 1,500 (keep 80-90%)
**Step 3**: AI for explanations, tutors for videos
**Step 4**: Student feedback drives refinement

**Output**: 1,500 verified questions + 100 videos in 3 weeks
**Cost**: ~₦50-75K
**Quality**: 90%+
