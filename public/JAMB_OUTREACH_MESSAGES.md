## ⚖️ Important Notes

1. **Always include opt-out**: "Reply STOP to unsubscribe"
2. **Be honest**: No spam, no fake claims
3. **Personal > Automated**: Real messages from real people work better
4. **Timing**: Send during school hours (3-5pm), not midnight
5. **Frequency**: Max 2 messages per week per channel
6. **A/B Test**: Try different messages, track what converts best
