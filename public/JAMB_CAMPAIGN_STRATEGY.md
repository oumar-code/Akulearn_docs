# JAMB 2026 Campaign Strategy

## 🎯 Campaign Overview

**Target**: JAMB 2026 Candidates (just registered or registering)  
**Offer**: Free Premium access during preparation period  
**Goal**: 
- Acquire 50,000+ JAMB users in 3 months
- Gather product feedback before national rollout
- Establish brand loyalty early
- Convert 10-15% to paid after exam season

---

## 📋 Campaign Timeline

### Phase 1: Launch (January 30 - February 15)
- Deploy JAMB-specific landing page (jamb.html - LIVE ✅)
- Share on social media platforms (Twitter, Instagram, TikTok)
- Post in JAMB WhatsApp groups & forums
- Target: 5,000 sign-ups

### Phase 2: Momentum (February 16 - March 31)
- Media coverage pitch (use existing press release)
- Influencer partnerships (popular JAMB YouTubers/TikTokers)
- SMS/WhatsApp campaigns to JAMB registrants
- Referral program (student refers friend = both get bonus questions)
- Target: 20,000 more sign-ups (25,000 cumulative)

### Phase 3: Scale (April - May)
- School partnerships (free classroom licenses)
- TV/Radio spots in major markets
- Feedback integration (rapid updates based on user input)
- Target: 25,000+ more sign-ups (50,000+ cumulative)
