📚 Mitosis vs Meiosis Comparison

Feature Mitosis Meiosis
Purpose Growth, repair, asexual reproduction Sexual reproduction, gamete formation
Number of Divisions One (1) Two (II)
Daughter Cells 2 identical cells 4 unique cells
Chromosome Number 2n → 2n (diploid) 2n → n (haploid)
Genetic Variation None (clones) Maximum (crossing over)
Where It Occurs Somatic cells (body) Germ cells (reproductive)
Duration 20-40 minutes Days to weeks
