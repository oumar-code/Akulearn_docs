# Regulatory & Compliance Checklist — Zamfara Pilot

## Telecom & Regulatory
- Contact NCC early: understand any notification or license requirements for local VoIP and eSIM trials
- Check spectrum usage rules for LoRa/mesh (sub-GHz) and Wi‑Fi (2.4/5 GHz)
- Register any base station equipment if required by national policy

## Safety & Electrical
- PV installation safety: comply with local electrical codes for PV array and battery installation
- Fire safety and battery containment requirements (LiFePO4 best practices)
- Wind turbine mast grounding: connect turbine tower and mast to earth via at least 16 mm² conductor
- DC surge protection devices (SPD, IEC 61643-31 Type II) on PV string and wind DC bus
- Dump-load / crowbar circuit required on wind controller — turbine must never run open-circuit
- All DC circuits ≥ 48 V must be labelled with voltage, polarity, and arc-flash warning
- Overcurrent protection (fuse or breaker) on every source branch (solar, wind) and load branch

## Environmental & Site Permissions
- Obtain written permission from school authorities for rooftop installations and from local community leaders if antennas extend coverage
- Document site-specific constraints and local community approvals

## Data Privacy & Protection
- Student data: obtain explicit parent/guardian consent where PII is collected or shared off-site
- Anonymize or pseudonymize student identifiers before aggregation to Super Hub
- Store data in encrypted form in transit and at rest

## Import / Customs and Procurement
- Confirm any import restrictions, customs tariffs, and documentation needed for shipping hardware to Nigeria
- Prepare commercial invoices and supplier declarations
