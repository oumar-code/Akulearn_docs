# Zakwan Lawali Dashboard — Skill Acquisition & Vocational Training

The Zakwan Lawali Dashboard is a dedicated interface for the **Head of Skill Acquisition & Vocational Training** role. Zakwan leads Akulearn's vocational and skill-acquisition programme — an initiative equivalent in ambition to the 3MTT programme but fully integrated within the Aku ecosystem and tailored to local needs.

## Key Features

- **Course Management:** Create, organise, and publish skill-acquisition courses covering technical trades, digital literacy, software development, and vocational disciplines.
- **Vocational Programmes:** Manage end-to-end vocational training tracks — enrolment, curriculum scheduling, assessments, and graduation milestones.
- **Trainee Progress:** Monitor individual and cohort progress in real time: attendance, assessment scores, completion rates, and skill certifications.
- **Certification Management:** Issue, track, and verify digital certificates upon course completion. Integrates with the Aku blockchain-backed credential system.
- **Partner Institutions:** Manage relationships with partner organisations, apprenticeship employers, government training bodies, and NGOs that co-deliver programmes.
- **Content Management:** Upload and curate training materials — videos, PDFs, quizzes, practical assessments, and offline-ready content packages.
- **Analytics:** View programme-level KPIs: enrolment trends, completion rates, employment outcomes, and geographic reach.
- **Aku Workspace:** Access to the shared Aku Workspace for cross-team collaboration, documents, and project tracking.
