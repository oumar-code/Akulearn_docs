# Balkisu Sani Kaura Dashboard — Finance & Content Management

The Balkisu Sani Kaura Dashboard is a dedicated interface for the **Head of Finance & Content Management** role. Balkisu oversees Akulearn's financial planning and reporting while also directing the platform's content strategy and editorial pipeline.

## Key Features

- **Budget Management:** Create and manage departmental budgets, track expenditure in real time, and produce variance reports against approved plans.
- **Financial Reporting:** Generate income statements, cash-flow summaries, grant disbursement reports, and investor financial updates.
- **Content Pipeline:** Oversee the end-to-end content production workflow — from commissioning and drafting through to editorial review and publication.
- **Content Approval:** Review and approve (or reject) content submitted by educators, trainers, and contributors before it goes live on the platform.
- **Content Management:** Direct access to the CMS for creating, editing, categorising, and archiving all platform content.
- **Analytics:** Monitor content performance metrics (views, completion, ratings) alongside financial KPIs on a single dashboard.
- **Audit Logs:** Read access to financial and content audit trails for compliance, governance, and reporting purposes.
- **Aku Workspace:** Access to the shared Aku Workspace for cross-team collaboration, documents, and project tracking.
