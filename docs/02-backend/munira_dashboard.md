# Munira Abubakar Dashboard — Product & External Engagement

The Munira Abubakar Dashboard is a dedicated interface for the **Head of Product & External Engagement** role. Munira drives Akulearn's product strategy, leads external-facing activities, manages social media presence, and serves as the face and voice of the platform.

!!! note "Upcoming"
    Munira has a product pitch scheduled for **mid-March 2026**. Pitch materials and investor decks are pinned to the top of this dashboard.

## Key Features

- **Product Roadmap:** Full read/write access to the live product roadmap — tracking features, milestones, and delivery timelines across all platform components.
- **Social Media Hub:** Centralised view of Akulearn's social channels (Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Instagram), scheduled posts, engagement analytics, and campaign performance.
- **Pitch Deck Management:** Access and update the latest investor and partnership pitch decks, keeping slides, narratives, and data current before every presentation.
- **Investor Deck:** Dedicated section for fundraising decks, financial projections, traction metrics, and due-diligence documents.
- **Demo Materials:** Curated library of live-demo scripts, product walkthroughs, video demos, and screen-capture assets ready for any audience.
- **Platform Overview:** Consolidated one-pager and product-summary views summarising Akulearn's mission, reach, and impact for quick reference during pitches.
- **Marketing Analytics:** Real-time data on platform growth, user adoption, regional reach, and media coverage to support data-driven storytelling.
- **Presentation Tools:** Integrated tools for slide editing, speaker-notes management, and rehearsal scheduling.
- **Media Kit:** Brand assets, approved messaging, logos, press releases, and media-contact lists for outreach and PR purposes.
- **Aku Workspace:** Access to the shared Aku Workspace for cross-team collaboration, documents, and project tracking.
