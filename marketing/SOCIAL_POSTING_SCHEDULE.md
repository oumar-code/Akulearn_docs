## Posting Best Practices

**Optimal Times** (LinkedIn data):
- **Weekdays**: 8-9 AM, 12-1 PM, 5-6 PM
- **Weekends**: 10-11 AM, 6-7 PM
- **Avoid**: 3-4 PM, late evening (after 9 PM)

**Engagement Boosters**:
- 2-3 sentences per line break (scannability)
- Emoji usage (1-2 per post for visual break)
- Hashtag strategy: 3-5 relevant tags (#Akulearn #NLP #EdTech #Nigeria #VersaTech)
- Link CTAs: Schedule consultation â†’ Calendly

**Tracking Metrics**:
- Daily impressions vs engagement rate
- Click-through rate to Calendly/portfolio
- Comment sentiment (B2B interest vs general interest)
- Follower growth rate
