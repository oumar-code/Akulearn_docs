Phase 1 of the Akulearn content population strategy has been successfully completed. All three major components have been implemented and tested:

**Files Created:**
- `encyclopedia_system.py` - Subject-specific encyclopedia management

**Subjects Covered:**
- Mathematics (Calculus, Probability, Linear Algebra)
- Physics (Newtonian Mechanics, Electromagnetism, Thermodynamics)
- Chemistry (Organic, Inorganic, Physical Chemistry)
- Biology (Cell Biology, Genetics, Ecology)

### 4. Flashcard System ✅

**Files Created:**
- `flashcard_system.py` - Spaced repetition flashcard system

**Implementation:**
- **SM-2 Algorithm:** Full spaced repetition implementation with ease factors, intervals, and review scheduling
- **60+ Flashcards:** Across 6 subjects (Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Economics, Geography)
- **Deck Management:** Individual decks for each subject with statistics tracking
- **Study Sessions:** Intelligent mixing of due and new cards

**Features:**
- Difficulty tracking (Easy, Good, Hard, Again)
- Progress analytics and mastery tracking
- Mobile-responsive design ready
- Offline capability structure
